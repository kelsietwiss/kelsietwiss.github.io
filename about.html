About Me

Artist and Programmer

Kelsie Twiss

Artist and Programmer

Hello! I'm Kelsie. I am the artist and owner of Wild Heart Art Shop, a handmade gift company founded in 2013. I am a self-taught artist specializing in acrylic painting, although I love to learn new techniques and experiment with different processes and materials. I have a passion for making. I am currently attending PDX Code Guild to learn different computer programming languages and expand my making abilities into technology. I am especially interested in game design, as the ability to contribute to a new world is intriguing and exciting to me. In my free time I enjoy hiking throughout the beautiful Pacific Northwest, reading (a recent favorite is "The Particular Sadness of Lemon Cake" by Aimee Bender), cooking new foods, and working in my garden.
